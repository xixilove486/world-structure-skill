# Structural Analysis Template

## 1. System Definition
- What is the system?
- What is inside it?
- What is outside it?
- What remains unclear?

## 2. Anchor
- What is it trying to preserve, achieve, or orbit around?
- Is the anchor clear, split, hollow, or replaced?

## 3. Main Constraints
- What limits this system most right now?
- Which constraints are structural rather than temporary?

## 4. Layers and Roles
- Who sustains continuity?
- Who drives change?
- Who links layers?
- Who blocks, extracts, or overloads the system?

## 5. Support Chains
- What resources, trust, legitimacy, memory, or energy keep it alive?
- Which support chains are stable?
- Which are fragile?

## 6. Disturbance / Drift / Entropy
- What repeatedly deforms the system?
- Is the issue local, systemic, external, or role-generated?
- Is the system drifting away from its anchor?

## 7. Stage
- formation
- expansion
- bottleneck
- transition
- stabilization
- rigidity / fragmentation / renewal attempt

## 8. Structural Judgment
Write 3-6 sentences.

## 9. Core Contradiction
Write 1-3 sentences.

## 10. Main Risk Direction
If nothing changes, what is the most likely structural failure mode?

## 11. Best Intervention Priority
What should be repaired first, and why?

## 12. Uncertainty / Alternatives
What do we still not know?
What other reading is possible?
